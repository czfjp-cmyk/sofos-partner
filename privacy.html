Privacy Policy

This is a completely static website. We do not collect or store any personal information from visitors — not your name, email, IP address, or any other data.

How we handle privacy

  • By operating a purely static website, we do not collect, store, or process any personal information whatsoever.
  • By not using any analytics tools, tracking scripts, or cookies, we do not track your browsing behaviour or identify you in any way.
  • You are entitled to visit every page anonymously with full confidence that no data about you is being recorded.
  • You are obliged to nothing — simply browsing the site imposes no obligations on you regarding data sharing.
  • By choosing to use the contact form, you voluntarily send a message directly to our email system; that message is handled with strict confidentiality and is not stored on this website.
