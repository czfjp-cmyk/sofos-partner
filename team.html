Founding Partner

Michael Tan

With over 20 years of experience advising international companies on China operations, Michael has deep expertise across market entry, joint ventures, M&A, restructuring, and technology-driven business. Previously at Clifford Chance and Taylor Wessing, he now leads Sofos & Partners with a focus on regulatory challenges, data compliance, and cross-border strategy. He chairs the Legal and Competition Working Group of the European Chamber of Commerce and serves as an arbitrator at multiple international centers. His extensive business circle network and advocacy experience provide clients with unmatched strategic advantage.

  • Cross-border investment and joint ventures
  • Compliance, investigation and data privacy (CIPP/E certified)
  • Capital market and portfolio management strategy
Founding Partner

Paul Gao

Paul brings strong litigation expertise, particularly in criminal defense and enforcement matters.
